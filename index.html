WHY ONOFLOT?

OnoFlot is DURABLE

Onoflot is The innovative mechanical water level sensing system makes onoflot pretty much simpler compared to our competitors. Technology senses water level with a counter weight and float ball. This is the most durable way of sensing water level and that is what makes us different

OnoFlot is FLEXIBLE

The technology used in Onoflot is formulated by ourselves and is applied for patent (Application No. 2031/CHE/2008). It is not affected by the size of the tank, capacity of the motor or the type of water to be pumped. Onoflot can be customized to meet your requirements.

OnoFlot is SAFE

Onoflot is It is made of high quality components and the sensing unit is corrosion free. Plastic float balls and brass weights are used in it. Onoflot also comes with a low voltage cut off system, which will not allow your pump to work at low voltage. Onoflot is equipped with the advanced “Motor Guard Technology(MGT)” which protects your water pump from dry run.
